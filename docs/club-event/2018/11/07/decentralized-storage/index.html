Decentralized Storage


Time: 07:00 PM on Wednesday, November 7, 2018
Location: Bruininks Hall 123

Join us TODAY to take a deep dive into distributed storage methods. We will discuss the pros and cons (mostly cons) of storing information on distributed systems, and we will cover some workarounds and other solutions to facilitate the integration of information with these distributed systems. Previous blockchain knowledge suggested but not required.

When? Today at 7:00 PM

Where? Bruininks Hall 123

Snacks provided.

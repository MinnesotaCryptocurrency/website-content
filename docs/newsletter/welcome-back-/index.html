In the News

Here are four noteworthy crypto related news articles:

Near 95% of Bitcoin volume is fake

A recent study suggests that almost 95% of Bitcoin spot volume is fake. The total, average daily volume listed on CoinMarketCap is more than $6 billion, while analysis shows that the true figure is closer to $270 million.

Half of founder of TRON’s followers are fake

TRON has quite the devoted following and its founder Justin Sun – has faced criticism over accusations of marketing chaff, propaganda, and fake followings.

Binance Lite rolled out to 1300 locations

Binance Lite, a service which would allow users across the world (starting in Australia) to purchase Bitcoin at local shops has just been rolled out to more than 1300 locations across Australia.

CBOE Halts Bitcoin Futures, Considering Conceding Market to CME

The CBOE Futures Exchange has decided to not add a bitcoin (XBT) futures contract for March. The move comes at time when bitcoin trading volumes have seen more than 80% declines since early 2018.
